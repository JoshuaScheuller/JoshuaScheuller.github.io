Hip Hop planet is a document that explains the styles of hip hop, and how Hip Hop can appeal to several different types of people. Regardless of race, sexuality, gender, anyone can enjoy hiphop. Hip Hop music is a melting pot of different art styles and because of this it can appeal to many types of people. Hip Hop artists also write songs about their background and the different roots of their culture which creates awareness of other cultures.
